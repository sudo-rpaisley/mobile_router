About

A local dashboard for managing network adapters and safely trying small red-team utilities.

What this dashboard does

  • Lists wired, wireless, VPN, virtual, and Bluetooth interfaces with live updates.
  • Shows adapter details, addresses, operating status, and wireless mode controls.
  • Scans nearby networks or devices from the matching adapter detail page.
  • Provides bounded demos for network scans, traceroute, port scans, and red-team labs.
